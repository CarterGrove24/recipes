Ingredients:
 - 1 1/2 lbs ground beef
 - 1     bag tater tots
 - 1     can condensed cream of mushroom soup
 - 1     can green beans, drained
 - 1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
 - 1/2   teaspoon salt
 - 1/2   teaspoon pepper

Directions:
  1. Preheat oven to 350Â° F.
  2. Brown beef, drain.
  3. Mix browned beef, salt, pepper, soup and green beans. Pour in casserole.
  4. Place tater tots on top of beef mixture. Then top off with cheddar cheese.
  5. Cover with foil and bake 45 minutes. Remove foil and bake 15 minutes.
  6. Serve immediately.
